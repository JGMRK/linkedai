Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026  ·  LinkedAI Chrome Extension

Short version: LinkedAI collects no personal data. Your API key and posts stay on your device. We have no servers, no database, and no analytics.

1. Who we are

LinkedAI is a Chrome browser extension that helps users write LinkedIn posts using the OpenAI API. The extension is developed and maintained by an independent developer ("we", "us").

Contact: hello@linkedai.app

2. What data we collect

We collect no personal data. LinkedAI has no backend servers, no user accounts, and no analytics. Nothing you type, generate, or configure is ever sent to us.

The only data the extension stores is saved locally in your own browser using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Your OpenAI API key (stored only in your browser, never transmitted to us)
  • Your language preference
  • Any LinkedIn post samples you paste into the "My Voice" field

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone but you.

3. How your OpenAI API key is used

When you generate post suggestions, your browser sends a request directly to OpenAI's API (api.openai.com) using your own API key. This request is made from your browser — not through any LinkedAI server. We never see, store, or transmit your API key.

Your use of the OpenAI API is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

4. LinkedIn

LinkedAI adds a button to the LinkedIn website interface but does not read, store, or transmit any content from your LinkedIn account. The extension only injects UI elements — it does not access your LinkedIn profile, connections, messages, or any other LinkedIn data.

5. Cookies and tracking

LinkedAI uses no cookies and no tracking technologies of any kind. We use no analytics tools (no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, nothing).

6. Third-party services

The only third-party service the extension communicates with is OpenAI (api.openai.com), and only when you actively click "Generate". This communication uses your own API key and is governed by OpenAI's own privacy policy and terms of service.

7. Data retention and deletion

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you are in full control. You can clear all LinkedAI data at any time by removing the extension from Chrome, or by clearing your browser's extension storage via Chrome's developer tools.

8. Children's privacy

LinkedAI is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@linkedai.app.
